Allow Only Rules
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
inbound rules
	All Connections from (group = Slytherin) to (group != Dumbledore1 and group != Dumbledore2)
	All Connections from (group = Gryffindor) to (group != Dumbledore1 and group != Dumbledore2)
outbound rules
	All Connections from (group = Slytherin) to (group != Dumbledore1 and group != Dumbledore2)
	All Connections from (group = Gryffindor) to (group != Dumbledore1 and group != Dumbledore2)
